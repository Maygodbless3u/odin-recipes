The dish is tasty

Ingredients

  • 2(8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2(15.25 ounce) cans white corn, drained
  • 2(14 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  1. Combine the cream cheese, butterm corn, tomatoes with green chile peppers in a slow cooker
  2. Set the cooker to Low
  3. Cook until the cream cheese and butter melt completely for 1 hour
